Frequently Asked Questions

What is WePay?
WePay is a secure blockchain-based platform for cryptocurrency transactions, designed to make transactions simple and efficient.
How does blockchain ensure security?
Blockchain uses cryptographic hashing and distributed consensus mechanisms to ensure secure, immutable transactions.
Is there a fee for transactions?
Yes, a minimal fee is charged to maintain the blockchain network and validate transactions securely.
How do I get started?
To get started, create an account, set up a wallet, and start making secure blockchain transactions through our platform.
Can I track my transactions?
Yes, you can track all your transactions in real time using our platform's transaction history feature.
What cryptocurrencies are supported?
WePay supports major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and many others. Visit our supported currencies page for the full list.
Is WePay available globally?
Yes, WePay is a global platform available in most countries, ensuring fast and secure transactions worldwide.
