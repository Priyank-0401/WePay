Frequently Asked Questions

What is WePay?

WePay is a secure blockchain-based payment platform that enables fast, secure, and transparent financial transactions using cryptocurrency and traditional payment methods.

How secure are my transactions?

All transactions on WePay are secured using advanced blockchain technology and encryption. We implement multiple layers of security, including two-factor authentication and real-time transaction monitoring.

What cryptocurrencies do you support?

We currently support major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and select stable coins. We regularly evaluate and add support for additional cryptocurrencies based on market demand and security considerations.

How long do transactions take?

Transaction times vary depending on the payment method and network conditions. Cryptocurrency transactions typically confirm within 10-60 minutes, while traditional payment methods process within 1-3 business days.

What are the transaction fees?

Our fee structure varies based on transaction type and volume. Cryptocurrency transactions incur network fees plus a small service fee, while traditional payment methods have standard processing fees. Check our pricing page for detailed information.
